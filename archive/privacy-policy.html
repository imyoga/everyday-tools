Privacy Policy

At Everyday Tools, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our web-based tools and visit our website.

Information We Don't Collect

Everyday Tools operates all processing directly in your browser. This means:

  • We do not store, transmit, or process your files on our servers
  • The files you upload or create remain entirely on your device
  • We have no access to the content of your documents or images
  • No account registration is required to use our tools

Information We Do Collect

We collect minimal anonymous usage data to help improve our services:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet)
  • Approximate geographic location (country-level only)
  • Pages visited and tools used (without any personal identifiers)
  • Referring website or source

Cookies and Local Storage

We use minimal cookies and local storage for functional purposes only:

  • To remember your preferences (like dark/light mode)
  • To enhance usability (like previous tool selections)
  • We do not use cookies for advertising purposes

Third-Party Services

We use limited third-party services to help operate our website:

  • Netlify (for website hosting)
  • Google Analytics (with anonymized IP addresses and minimal tracking)
  • No third parties have access to your uploaded files

Children's Privacy

Our services are not designed for or directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our social media channels.
