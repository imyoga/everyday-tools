About Everyday Tools

Everyday Tools is a collection of web-based utilities designed to simplify common digital tasks. Our mission is to provide free, accessible, and privacy-focused tools that operate entirely within your browser.

Founded in 2023, we recognized the need for straightforward, no-nonsense utilities that don't require downloads, installations, or account registrations. Our growing collection includes tools for document management, image editing, development, and more.

Our Core Principles:

  • Privacy First: All processing happens in your browser. We never store or access your files.
  • No Limitations: No arbitrary usage limits or paywalls for basic functionality.
  • Accessibility: Tools that work across devices and browsers with minimal requirements.
  • Simplicity: Straightforward interfaces focused on getting the job done.

Everyday Tools is maintained by a small team of developers passionate about creating useful web utilities. We're constantly working to improve existing tools and add new ones based on user feedback.

Have a suggestion for a new tool or an improvement to an existing one? We'd love to hear from you! Reach out through any of our social channels.
